Riser

A variometer for paraglider pilots. Real-time lift and sink data on your Pixel Watch — no extra hardware needed.

Riser turns your Wear OS watch into a precision variometer. It uses the built-in barometric sensor to deliver Kalman-filtered vertical speed with sub-second response times, plus audio and haptic feedback so you can keep your eyes on the sky.

The companion Android app automatically syncs your flights from the watch and lets you export FAI-standard IGC files for sharing, analysis, or competition logging.
